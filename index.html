INSIDE
THE BUILD

🔋

BATTERY PACK

8× Grade A prismatic LFP cells (100Ah each) in a 4S2P configuration — delivering 12V at 200Ah. Architecture is already designed for upgrade to 8S1P at 24V when the hybrid inverter arrives.

4S2P12V / 200AhLFP Chemistry
🧠

SMART BMS

JK Smart BMS (4–8S, 200A) with 2A active cell balancing continuously redistributes energy to keep SOC (State of Charge) uniform across the pack — critical for cycle life and preventing cell-level over/under-charge.

JK 200A BMS2A Active BalancingDual NTC Probes
☀️

SOLAR ARRAY

~1.1kW bifacial solar panels feeding a 50A MPPT charge controller. MPPT continuously tracks the panel's peak power point — maximising harvest regardless of temperature variation or partial shading.

1.1kW Peak50A MPPTAshapower
⚡

INVERTER & DISPLAY

Microtek 12V 1kW Pure Sine Wave inverter delivers clean waveform output to household loads. JK 3.2" SOC display shows real-time voltage, current, State of Charge %, and pack temperature at a glance.
